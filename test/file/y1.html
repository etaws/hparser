      Review from Judy C.

      •  
      • 38 friends

      • Judy C.

      Oakland, CA

      1/31/2011

      My BFF and I wanted to get tattoos together. This was going to be my second one, and I'm hella anal about who I let stick a needle in me. The place I wanted to get our tattoos at were pretty booked, so my BFF (who had gotten some work done at Moth and Dagger before) got in touch with JD. He responded right away that he had an opening that following Monday, so that's what I spent the weekend mentally preparing for.

      Upon entering the studio, I was introduced to JD and Mario. It's a small shop, but don't let the size fool you. These guys are extremely trained professionals and utilize the space well. It's also very well-lit and clean, and the guys are very welcoming. Right away you can tell that these guys are all about being honest with you and giving you what you want if they can. So it didn't come as a surprise when the other people entering the shop were obviously repeat costumers and the verbal rapport between everyone was lighthearted, witty, and hilarious.

      My BFF's tattoo took about 45 minutes and it turned out great. Because of the location of where he got it, JD instructed the BFF to check in with him in a couple weeks in case it needed to be touched up and the lines straightened after it's healed. I thought it a great customer service that he offered.

      My tattoo only took 25 minutes and I am extremely pleased with how it turned out. JD gave me great advice on how to deal with the healing (the itching was the worst). I love that he makes himself available and is pretty prompt about his responses in case I had a concern after leaving the shop. And what he charged me was really reasonable.

      Heads up, they're cash only. But they totally will let you leave if you need to find an ATM. That kind of trust is unrivaled. I will definitely go back or recommend to anyone!

    Moth and Dagger is a small Tattoo Studio conveniently located in the Downtown San Francisco Area. Close to many San Francisco landmarks and tourist attractions; including the Historical Cable Car system, the China Town Gates and beautiful Union Square.

    The studio is operated by Mario and Justin. Both are well rounded artists who can tackle most any subject matter or style.

    Established in 2009

    Opened fall 2009

    Meet the Business Owner: Mario D.

    Mario is an Alumni from The Academy of Art University studying Illustration for his BFA. He has lived in San Francisco since 2003 when he moved here for school.

    He specializes in Illustrative, portraits, Black&Gray and Color realism
